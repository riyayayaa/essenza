Frida Kahlo

Mexican Painter, Surrealist Symbolist

Frida Kahlo is one of the most celebrated Latin American artists, known for her emotionally raw self-portraits and exploration of identity, pain, and womanhood. Her deeply personal style merged indigenous Mexican traditions with surrealist elements. Despite lifelong health struggles, she used art to express resilience, vulnerability, and complex emotion.
